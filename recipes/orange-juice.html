Orange Juice

Description:

Another important part of your balanced breakfast, orange juice is a fantastic way to stave off scurvy.

Ingredients:

  • A single big orange (per 8 oz)

Steps:

  1. Procure a glass and place it on a flat, stable surface.
  2. Take your orange, ensuring the juice will pour into the glass, smash it against your eye.
  3. One orange should fill a standard 8 oz glass

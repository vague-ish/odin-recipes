Toast!

Description:

An integral part of a balanced breakfast, who doesn't like three slices of toast? A slick metal holder adds some extra iron for those of us whose blood may not be up to snuff!

Ingredients:

  • Three slices of your favorite bread. It must be three exactly or none of this works, no substitutes.
  • Metal toast rack
  • (Optional) Topping of choice

Steps:

  1. Place toast in toaster and set the toaster to your preferred toasting time.
  2. While awaiting the toast to toast, prep your metal holder by rinsing it and then placing it on a counter. Drying is optional.
  3. Toast is ready when it pops out. It's extra ready if it's launched into the ceiling or maybe your eye.
  4. Place toast in rack
  5. (Optional) Drench the toast and holder with your topping of choice.
  6. Voila! Your toast is ready. Best eaten by unhinging your jaw and consuming everything at once.
